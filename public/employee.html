PTO Note

All PTO requests must be approved by your direct supervisor in advance. Please note that there are peak periods—particularly from July 15 to September 15, which coincide with the start of the school year—during which PTO may be limited or not approved due to operational needs.

Additionally, employees are not eligible to use PTO within the first 90 days of employment and the use of your full PTO allowance within the first six months of employment may not be permitted. Approval will be based on business needs and manager discretion. Our PTO bucket is a combined pool for both sick days and personal days off. We do not have separate buckets, so you can use your PTO as needed for either type of absence.

Paid Holidays

    Holiday Observance

      Bereavement Leave

      • SchoolsPLP recognizes the importance of taking leave when there is a death in the family. You are entitled to take up to 3 days off with pay for the funeral of an immediate relative. Pay is based on the regular rate for an eight-hour day. Authorized leave without pay is available for extended funeral matters. Personal leave time may also be taken when necessary. Notify Human Resources of your intention to take bereavement leave as soon as the need arises. The Company may request documentation to support absences for bereavement leave.

      Rollover Policy

      • Unused PTO carries over into your next work year, with a maximum rollover of 3 days (24 hours).
